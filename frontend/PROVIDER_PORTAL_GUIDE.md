# Provider Portal - Purpose & Features

## Overview
The **Provider Portal** is a dedicated interface for healthcare providers (doctors, clinicians) to manage their clinical practice and patient interactions within the DocAI platform.

## Purpose
The Provider Portal enables healthcare providers to:
- **Manage Patient Appointments** - View, schedule, and manage appointments
- **Review Patient Records** - Process incoming medical records and documents from patients
- **Communicate with Patients** - Send and receive messages from patients
- **Monitor Triage Data** - Track high-priority and urgent patient cases
- **Access Patient Charts** - View detailed patient information and medical history

## Key Sections

### 1. **Dashboard**
- **Quick Overview** - Total appointments, high-priority cases, video visits, pending lab reviews
- **Appointment Queue** - Shows today's scheduled appointments with patient details
- **AI Triage Integration** - Displays AI-generated summaries of patient conditions
- **Quick Actions** - Quick access to patient details and appointment management

### 2. **Appointments (Calendar & Scheduling)**
- **Weekly Calendar View** - Visual calendar showing all scheduled appointments
- **Appointment Types** - Distinguishes between video visits and in-person consultations
- **Priority Indicators** - Highlights high-priority patient appointments
- **Appointment Details** - Shows patient name, reason for visit, and appointment type

### 3. **Messages**
- **Patient Communication** - Secure messaging with patients
- **Message History** - View previous conversations with patients
- **Conversation List** - Quick access to all active conversations with notification badges
- **Direct Messaging** - Send medical advice and follow-up instructions to patients

### 4. **Incoming Records Queue**
- **Prescription Review** - Analyze uploaded prescriptions
- **Lab Results** - Review laboratory test results from patients
- **Document Processing** - Process medical documents uploaded by patients
- **AI Analysis** - Automated extraction of key medical data
- **Alert System** - Flags potential drug interactions and anomalies

## User Profile
The Provider Portal is designed for:
- Primary Care Physicians
- Specialists
- Cardiologists
- Other Licensed Healthcare Providers

## Integration with Patient Portal
- **Two-way Communication** - Patients can message providers through the Patient Portal's Messages section
- **Unified System** - Provider and patient portals are interconnected

- **Appointment Notifications** - Appointments created in provider portal notify patients

## Typical Workflow
1. Provider logs into Portal
2. Checks Dashboard for today's overview and high-priority cases
3. Reviews appointment queue and prepares for consultations
4. Responds to patient messages throughout the day
5. Reviews incoming records and prescriptions
6. Updates patient charts and schedules follow-ups
7. Logs patient interactions and notes

---

**Related Component**: The Chat feature added to the Patient Portal allows patients to message their providers, whose responses appear in the Provider Portal's Messages section.
